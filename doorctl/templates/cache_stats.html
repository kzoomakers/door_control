Cache Management

Cache Information

The cache stores expensive REST API responses to improve page load performance:

  • Controllers List: Cached for 30 minutes
  • Card Lists: Cached for 30 minutes per controller
  • Card Details: Cached for 30 minutes per card
  • Time Profiles: Cached for 30 minutes per controller
  • Door States: Cached for 5 minutes per controller
  • Global Cards Data: Cached for 30 minutes

Note: Cache is automatically invalidated when you add, edit, or delete cards or time profiles.
